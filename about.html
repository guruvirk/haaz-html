About Us

Get to Know Us

Haaz Forwarding & Logistics was founded in 2019 under dynamic and experienced leadership of Mr. Sumit Dixit. Haaz Forwarding & Logistics is a Freight Forwarding & Logistics company situated in New Zealand. We have offices in Auckland and Christchurch. We specialise in Import, Export, Custom Clearance, and Container Movement.

Haaz Forwarding & Logistics cater many clients globally with major clients in Pacific, Asia and Middle East regions.
